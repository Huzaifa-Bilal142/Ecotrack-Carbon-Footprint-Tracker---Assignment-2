Our Mission

EcoTrack was created to empower individuals to understand and reduce their environmental impact. We believe that meaningful climate action starts with awareness, and that small changes by many people can lead to significant positive change.

Our Story

EcoTrack began as a university project by a group of environmentally-conscious computer science students. Frustrated by the lack of accessible tools to help individuals understand their personal impact on climate change, we set out to create a simple yet powerful carbon footprint calculator.

What started as a class assignment has grown into a passion project with the goal of making carbon tracking approachable and actionable for everyone. We continue to improve the platform based on user feedback and the latest climate science.

While we're a small team, we're committed to providing accurate information and practical solutions to help reduce individual carbon footprints.
